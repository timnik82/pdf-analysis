## KEY TAKEAWAYS FROM VERIFIED RESEARCH:

### Thermal Conductivity Enhancements:
- Graphene aerogels: 0.6-3.2 W·m⁻¹·K⁻¹ (2-16× enhancement over pure PCMs)
- CNT composites: 0.5-1.5 W·m⁻¹·K⁻¹
- Hybrid carbon structures: Up to 3.2 W·m⁻¹·K⁻¹

### Latent Heat Retention:
- Most composites: 140-210 J·g⁻¹
- Best performance: >200 J·g⁻¹ with >95% retention after 500 cycles

### Cycling Stability:
- Standard: 100-200 cycles
- Advanced: 500-1000+ cycles with <5% performance loss

### Applications:
1. Solar thermal energy storage
2. Building thermal management
3. Battery thermal management
4. Wearable devices
5. Electronics cooling
